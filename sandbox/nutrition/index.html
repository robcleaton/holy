Storage & Usage

Storage instructions

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Do not refrigerate. Once opened, reseal the tub tightly and use within 3 months.

How to prepare

Add one level scoop (12.5 g) to 500 ml of cold water. Shake or stir well until fully dissolved. Best served chilled over ice. Do not heat.

Important caution

  • Not suitable during pregnancy
