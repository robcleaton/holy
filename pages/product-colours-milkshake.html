Product Colours

Milkshake

Colour variables for all HOLY Milkshake products. Each product has two values — /1 (light) and /2 (dark/saturated) — defined in the Products Figma variable collection.

Variable convention: --milkshake/{ProductName}/1 and --milkshake/{ProductName}/2
e.g. --milkshake/Strawberry/1 → #FBABD4
