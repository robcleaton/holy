Product Colours

Syrup

Colour variables for all HOLY Syrup products. Each product has two values — /1 (light) and /2 (dark/saturated) — defined in the Products Figma variable collection.

Variable convention: --syrup/{productName}/1 and --syrup/{productName}/2
e.g. --syrup/mango/1 → #FFDAA5
