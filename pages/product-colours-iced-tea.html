Product Colours

Iced Tea

Colour variables for all HOLY Iced Tea products. Each product has two values — /1 (light) and /2 (dark/saturated) — defined in the Products Figma variable collection.

Variable convention: --iced-tea/{ProductName}/1 and --iced-tea/{ProductName}/2
e.g. --iced-tea/Peach/1 → #F5A488
