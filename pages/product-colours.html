Product Colours

Colour variables for each product across all HOLY ranges. Each product has two values — /1 (light) and /2 (dark/saturated) — defined in the Products Figma variable collection.

Variable convention: --{category}/{ProductName}/1 and --{category}/{ProductName}/2
e.g. --energy/AcaiAnaconda/1 → #CF7CC5
