Product Colours

Hydration

Colour variables for all HOLY Hydration products. Each product has two values — /1 (light) and /2 (dark/saturated) — defined in the Products Figma variable collection.

Variable convention: --hydration/{ProductName}/1 and --hydration/{ProductName}/2
e.g. --hydration/Watermelon/1 → #F8C9DF
