Product Colours

Energy

Colour variables for all HOLY Energy products. Each product has two values — /1 (light) and /2 (dark/saturated) — defined in the Products Figma variable collection.

Variable convention: --energy/{ProductName}/1 and --energy/{ProductName}/2
e.g. --energy/AcaiAnaconda/1 → #CF7CC5
